When a customer access Mikkeller Webshop platform and they may browse the products in the webshop. A customer must browse for products prior to adding a product to the basket, but they can access the basket upon entering the webshop without any prior conditions. A customer may adjust the quantity of the products prior to adding the product to the basket, but they could also add a product with its default quantity of 1. A customer may adjust the quantity or remove products within the basket. In order to proceed to check-out at least one product must be in the basket. In order to complete the purchase, a customer must fill correct contact, shipping and billing details. The payment method used in the billing details must have sufficient funds to cover the order, otherwise the payment will be rejected. At any time the customer may leave the webshop page.
